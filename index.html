How It Works

Step 1: Enter Subject Name

Type the name of your subject in the Subject Name field. For example: Web Development, Python, or Java.

1 of 4

Step 2: Enter Your Grade

Enter a number from 0 to 100 for your grade. If the input is invalid the form will show an error alert.

2 of 4

Step 3: Click Add Grade

Press the Add Grade button or hit Enter on your keyboard. Your grade will appear in the list with a remark automatically.

3 of 4

Step 4: See Your Average

Your overall average is computed automatically and shown in the box on the right. Add more subjects to see it update live!
